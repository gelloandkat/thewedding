Our story

Our story began in the most natural way—at work. What started as a simple introduction through a common friend turned into something we never expected. Out of curiosity and a little courage, we decided to give it a try—and from that moment, everything just felt right. As days turned into months, we found ourselves growing closer. Conversations became deeper, laughter became more frequent, and the connection we once took a chance on slowly turned into something real. We didn’t just get to know each other—we found comfort, support, and happiness in one another. Somewhere along the way, without even realizing it, we fell in love. Now, here we are—ready to take the next step, hand in hand, choosing each other for a lifetime. What started with a simple introduction has led us to this beautiful moment, and we can’t wait to continue writing our story together. This is the beginning of our forever.
